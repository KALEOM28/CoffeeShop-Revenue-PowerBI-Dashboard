# ☕ Coffee Sales Performance & Revenue Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a data-driven approach to understanding customer purchasing behavior and sales performance for a coffee shop retail business. Leveraging a dataset of daily coffee transactions, I built an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** to uncover revenue trends, peak operational hours, and product popularity.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Used
- **Business Intelligence:** Power BI (Desktop & Service)
- **Data Engineering:** Power Query (Data Cleansing, Handling Missing Values, Date/Time Transformations)
- **Data Source:** `Coffe_sales.csv` (Detailed records of transaction dates, coffee names, pricing, and payment methods)

## 🔍 Key Business Insights Explored
- **Hourly Sales Distribution:** Identified peak customer rush periods (Morning vs. Afternoon vs. Evening) to optimize staff scheduling and inventory preparation.
- **Product Mix Performance:** Ranked top-selling coffee types (e.g., Latte, Americano, Hot Chocolate) by sales volume and profitability.
- **Payment Mode Preference:** Tracked the percentage ratio of Cash vs. Card transactions to streamline counter-billing efficiency.
- **Temporal Analysis:** Visualized revenue trends across different Weekdays and Months to spot seasonal sales spikes.

## 🚀 Strategic Business Recommendations
1. **Resource Allocation:** Increase barista staff during identified peak hours to minimize customer wait time and prevent revenue loss.
2. **Targeted Promotions:** Run bundled discounts or happy hour offers during slow operational hours (e.g., late evenings) to boost foot traffic.
3. **Inventory Planning:** Align raw material ordering (milk, specific coffee beans, cocoa) directly with the highest-performing product volumes shown in the dashboard.
